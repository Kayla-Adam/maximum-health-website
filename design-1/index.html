What We Help With

Whether you're dealing with chronic pain, recovering from injury, or simply seeking relief from everyday stress, our team provides personalized therapeutic care to help you feel your best.

⚫

Back, Neck & Shoulder Pain

Targeted therapeutic massage to release tension, correct postural imbalances, and restore comfortable movement in these commonly affected areas.
⚫

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, rhythmic massage that stimulates your lymphatic system to reduce swelling, boost immune function, and support post-surgical recovery.
⚫

Sports Injuries

Recover faster and prevent re-injury with targeted rehabilitation massage designed for active individuals and athletes.
⚫

Chronic Tension

Break the cycle of persistent muscle tightness with whole-body assessment and personalized treatment plans.
⚫

Mobility Restrictions

Restore range of motion through myofascial release, joint mobilization, and targeted stretching techniques.
⚫

Pre & Post Natal Massage

Specialized care for expecting and new mothers, addressing pregnancy-related discomfort and supporting postpartum recovery with safe, adapted techniques.

Calgary's Trusted Wellness Centre

For nearly three decades, Maximum Health Wellness Centre has been helping Calgarians live with less pain and more freedom. Located in the heart of NW Calgary near Kensington, our multi-disciplinary clinic brings together registered massage therapists, acupuncturists, osteopaths, and more — all under one roof.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptom. Every treatment begins with listening, understanding your goals, and building a plan that empowers you to take control of your health.
