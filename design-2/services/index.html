Comprehensive Wellness Services in Calgary

At Maximum Health Wellness Centre, we offer a wide range of services tailored to your unique needs — from registered massage therapy to acupuncture, traditional Chinese medicine, and osteopathy. Whether you are seeking relief from chronic pain, recovering from an injury, managing pregnancy-related discomfort, or simply looking to relax and recharge, our multi-disciplinary team has the skills and experience to help. Every treatment is personalized based on a thorough assessment, so you receive the care that is right for your body.

⚫

Swedish Massage

Classic relaxation massage using long, flowing strokes to ease tension, improve circulation, and promote overall well-being. Ideal for first-time clients and stress relief.
⚫

Deep Tissue Therapeutic

Firm pressure targeting deeper muscle layers and connective tissue to address chronic tension, adhesions, and persistent pain for lasting relief.
⚫

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, rhythmic technique that stimulates your lymphatic system to reduce swelling, support immune function, and promote natural detoxification.
⚫

Pre & Post Natal

Specialized massage for expecting and new mothers, addressing pregnancy-related discomfort and supporting postpartum recovery with safe, adapted techniques.
⚫

TMJ & Facial Massage

Targeted treatment for jaw tension, TMJ disorders, and facial pain. Relieves teeth grinding effects, tension headaches, and jaw dysfunction.
⚫

Myofascial Release

Sustained pressure applied to fascial restrictions to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve the structural alignment of your body.
⚫

Sports Injury & Rehab

Targeted rehabilitation massage for athletes and active individuals to accelerate recovery, prevent re-injury, and optimize performance.
⚫

Mindful Massage

A calming, presence-focused approach that combines therapeutic touch with nervous system regulation to reduce stress, anxiety, and mental fatigue.
⚫

Pediatric Massage

Gentle, age-appropriate massage therapy for children and adolescents, supporting healthy development, easing growing pains, and reducing stress.
⚫

Osteopathy & Alignment Therapy

Holistic hands-on approach combining alignment assessment, myofascial release, medicupping, and more to restore proper function and encourage healthy movement.
⚫

Acupuncture & TCM

Traditional Chinese Medicine including acupuncture, NAET, and integrative modalities for fertility, allergies, mental health, pain, and overall wellness.
