About Maximum Health

Our Story

Maximum Health Wellness Centre has been serving the Calgary community for over 28 years. Located in NW Calgary near Kensington at 213 19 St NW #4, we offer a multi-disciplinary approach to wellness that includes registered massage therapy, traditional Chinese medicine, acupuncture, osteopathy, alignment therapy, and more.

What started as a single-practitioner clinic has grown into a trusted wellness destination, helping thousands of Calgarians reduce pain, improve mobility, and reclaim their quality of life. Our commitment to personalized, evidence-informed care has remained at the heart of everything we do.

Our Philosophy

At Maximum Health, we take a whole-person approach to wellness. We don't just treat symptoms — we work to understand the root cause of your pain or discomfort and develop a plan that supports lasting change.

Our philosophy is built on three core principles:

  • Nervous system regulation: We focus on calming and retraining your nervous system so your body can heal more effectively.
  • Long-term outcomes: Quick fixes aren't our style. We build treatment plans designed for lasting relief and functional improvement.
  • Empowering clients: We give you the tools, knowledge, and home care strategies to take an active role in your own healing.

We believe the body has an incredible capacity to adapt and heal when given the right support. Our job is to provide that support — through skilled hands-on treatment, thoughtful assessment, and genuine care for every client who walks through our doors.
