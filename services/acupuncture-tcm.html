Acupuncture & TCM

Traditional Chinese Medicine including acupuncture, NAET, and integrative modalities for fertility, allergies, mental health, pain, and overall wellness.

Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) has been practiced for over 3,000 years as a comprehensive system of healing that views the body, mind, and spirit as deeply interconnected. Rather than focusing on isolated symptoms, TCM seeks to identify and address the underlying imbalances that give rise to illness and discomfort. At Maximum Health Massage Therapy Calgary, our TCM treatments are built on the foundation of acupuncture, with additional modalities blended in to provide a truly integrative approach to your health.

Whether you are seeking relief from chronic pain, support through a fertility journey, help managing anxiety or allergies, or simply a path toward greater overall wellness, our practitioner creates a personalized treatment plan that draws on the full depth of Traditional Chinese Medicine to meet you where you are.

Treatment Modalities

Each treatment at Maximum Health is personalized to your unique health picture. Depending on your assessment and goals, your session may include one or more of the following modalities:

  • Acupuncture: Fine, sterile needles are placed at specific points along the body's meridians to restore the flow of energy (Qi), reduce pain, and stimulate healing. An AcuLaser non-needle option is also available for those who prefer needle-free treatment.
  • NAET (Nambudripad's Allergy Elimination Technique): A non-invasive method that uses a combination of energy balancing, testing, and treatment to address allergies and sensitivities at their root.
  • BodyTalk: A consciousness-based health care system that identifies and addresses weaknesses in communication between the body's systems.
  • Neural Emotional Technique (NET): A mind-body approach that helps resolve the physical effects of unresolved emotional patterns and stress.
  • Functional Medicine: An evidence-based approach that looks at the underlying causes of disease through comprehensive assessment and targeted interventions.
  • Root Cause Protocol: A systematic approach to restoring mineral balance and supporting the body's innate ability to heal.
  • Nutritional Coaching: Personalized dietary guidance to support your treatment goals and promote long-term health from the inside out.

Conditions We Treat

  • Fertility & Pregnancy: Optimizing fertility for men and women, morning sickness, back and hip pain during pregnancy, recurrent miscarriages, labor preparation, and postpartum depression and recovery.
  • Mental Health: Anxiety, depression, PTSD, mood swings, and ongoing stress management.
  • Pain & Injury: Acute and chronic pain conditions, repetitive strain injuries, and recovery support.
  • Allergies & Sensitivities: Food, environmental, seasonal, chemical, and mold sensitivities identified and treated through NAET and complementary approaches.
  • Sleep Issues: Insomnia and sleep quality improvement through nervous system regulation and meridian balancing.
  • Children's Health: Gentle, age-appropriate treatments for constipation, ear infections, eczema, headaches, allergies, and childhood anxiety.
