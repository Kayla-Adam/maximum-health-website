About Tracy

Tracy has been practicing massage therapy since 1999 and has been owner/operator of Maximum Health Wellness Centre in West Hillhurst since 2003. When she purchased the clinic, she was working in a large corporate setting where care felt fast-paced and transactional — and she knew something was missing. Her clients needed more than quick treatments. They needed time, attention, and genuine connection. Maximum Health was her chance to build the kind of clinic she had always envisioned: one where people felt seen, listened to, and supported, and where care was never rushed.

Tracy started with just two treatment rooms and a clear intention to build something personal, sustainable, and grounded in real relationships. Over the years, she built her clientele through long-term corporate contracts — including a 10-year partnership with ConocoPhillips Canada and multi-year engagements with companies like Smed (now Haworth) and Penn West. Many of the clients she met through those early contracts still see her today, which says everything about the kind of trust and consistency she brings to her work.

Beyond corporate wellness, Tracy has worked with elite athletes at the highest levels of sport. She was part of the massage therapy team for Team Canada Women's Hockey leading up to the 2006 Torino Olympics, providing treatment at practices and supporting athletes through their training season. She has also worked with carded bobsledders and speed skaters training for Team Canada and Team USA, as well as ex-CFL players and a former Team Canada downhill ski racer.

Tracy has also volunteered her skills at events including the Victoria Marathon, races in Canmore, the Calgary Women's Race, and traveled to Arizona to support decathletes training with the UFC Athletic Club. Her treatment style is a combination of deep therapeutic massage and relaxation, and she is confident treating a wide variety of client needs — from injury rehabilitation to stress management to preventative care.
